# Quickstart

Get osu! up and running quickly and start playing your first beatmap.

## Download and install

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your platform">
    Select the appropriate download for your operating system:

    <CardGroup cols={3}>
      <Card title="Windows" icon="windows" href="https://github.com/ppy/osu/releases/latest/download/install.exe">
        Windows 10+ (x64)
      </Card>
      <Card title="macOS" icon="apple" href="https://github.com/ppy/osu/releases/latest">
        macOS 12+ (Intel & Apple Silicon)
      </Card>
      <Card title="Linux" icon="linux" href="https://github.com/ppy/osu/releases/latest/download/osu.AppImage">
        Linux x64 AppImage
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    <Info>
      Mobile versions are also available for [iOS (TestFlight)](https://osu.ppy.sh/home/testflight) and [Android](https://github.com/ppy/osu/releases/latest/download/sh.ppy.osulazer.apk).
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run the installer">
    After downloading, run the installer for your platform:

    **Windows**: Run `install.exe` and follow the installation wizard.

    **macOS**: Extract the zip file and drag osu!.app to your Applications folder.

    **Linux**: Make the AppImage executable and run it:

    ```bash
    chmod +x osu.AppImage
    ./osu.AppImage
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch osu!">
    Start osu! for the first time. The game will:
    - Perform initial setup and download required resources
    - Guide you through basic configuration (audio, graphics, input)
    - Prompt you to create or log in to your account
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up for a free osu! account to:
    - Save your scores and statistics
    - Compete on global leaderboards
    - Play multiplayer with others
    - Track your performance over time

    <Note>
      You can play offline without an account, but scores won't be submitted to leaderboards.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Play your first beatmap

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse beatmaps">
    Click **Play** from the main menu to open the song selection screen.

    If you're new, you can download beatmaps directly in-game:
    1. Click the **Download beatmaps** button
    2. Browse or search for songs you like
    3. Click to download and import
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select difficulty">
    Each beatmap has multiple difficulty levels:
    - **Easy**: Perfect for beginners
    - **Normal**: Standard difficulty
    - **Hard**: Challenging gameplay
    - **Insane/Expert**: For experienced players

    Start with an Easy or Normal difficulty to learn the basics.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start playing">
    Press **Enter** or click the beatmap to start playing.

    **Basic controls (osu!standard mode)**:
    - Click circles with `Z` and `X` keys (or mouse clicks)
    - Follow sliders by holding down the key
    - Spin spinners by moving your cursor in circles

    <Tip>
      You can customize key bindings in **Settings** → **Input** → **Keyboard**.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="View your results">
    After completing a beatmap, you'll see your:
    - Score and accuracy
    - Combo count
    - Grade (F, D, C, B, A, S, SS)
    - Performance points (PP) if you're playing ranked beatmaps

    Your score will be submitted to the leaderboard if you're logged in and playing a ranked map.
  </Step>
</Steps>
